styling tips—

1. Boxy meets baby tee

Let your outerwear do the talking. Pair a bold jacket or structured blazer with a simple, minimal inner top—like a tank or fitted tee. This contrast draws the eye to your silhouette and gives your outfit a clean, confident edge.

2. The Sandwich Method

This styling trick plays with balance—matching the top and bottom pieces in color or tone while letting a contrasting piece shine in the middle. Think of it like a fashion sandwich: the “bread” (jacket and shoes) frames the “filling” (your top or pants), creating a cohesive, intentional look without much effort.

3. Layers on layers

Layering isn't just for warmth—it's a creative way to add depth and personality to your outfit. Mix textures, fabrics, and silhouettes to build a look that feels dynamic and styled. It's especially great for transitioning between seasons or elevating basics. Simplest way to start is to layer a skirt on top of a pair of pants for an interesting outfit dynamic.
